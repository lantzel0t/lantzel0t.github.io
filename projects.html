Project Drillbitclose

An open tower-defence game set within an alien cave system. Fight off wave after wave of bug monsters while protecting your resource collectors.
I spent a lot of my time in the project working on the procedural cave system, which allowed for mesh editing on the fly. The enemy wave system was also where much of my time was spent, as it uses a 'tremor' sensor to both inform the player of approaching enemies and keep track of time in and out of waves.

Project Ignitionclose

In this game, your fuel is your weapon. Drop pools of flammable gasoline behind you, and then run back over it to set it ablaze! Be sure to keep an eye on your gas gauge and find a gas pump when it gets too low. With a Mad Max inspired theme and Tron-like game-play, driving through a scarred wasteland has never been more fun.
All tech artists think sprite stacking looks neat when they first see it, and this was my attempt to make something using the technique. In retrospect, the style would be more viable by just rendering the top faces of a voxelized model to prevent overdraw. I am still very proud of how the tires accurately rotate with the turn of the car.

Outlast Islandclose

A co-op or single-player survival game with four different playable characters. Stranded on an island, you must fortify yourself during the day from the creatures that come out at night. This game was developed for a month-long gamejam, with myself as the sole developer. It features a full building system, algorithm-generated enemy waves, four different playable characters, six different monster types, and a levelling system. I challenged myself here by not using any external assets, with all music, pixel art, and programming being done in-house.

HipHopperclose

Hip Hopper is a first person platformer inspired by mascot platformers of the N64/PS1/PS2 era. The idea was to take a 3D Mario open world and reimagine it with the movement mechanics of Mirrors Edge. The objective is to make a demo in which we showcase the movement mechanics, how they play into the design of the world, and an original hip hop score to tie it together. Hip Hopper features wall jumping, wall climbing, and wall running in an interconnected world.
